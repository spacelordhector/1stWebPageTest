To Be or Not to Be: That is the Question

The Beginning of the End: Or Something Cool

When I was little I used to think that I was always to going to be a hero, saving people and getting all the praise. However, now that I'm 25 years old, in my mid-tweenties, I question my self if can even save the people around me.

The one major focus in my life, currently, is getting a job. Why? Because I believe that money will make my goals and dreams a reality. Of course this seems obvious because, sadly, the world does revolve around money. Not saying that money is everything; there are things beyond money but money does help.[1]

With that being said, here are a list of dreams I have:

  • Support my family
  • Have a wife
  • Raise my kids and dogs
  • Have meaning in life
